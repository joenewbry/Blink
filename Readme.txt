Blink - Get more from the people around you

Created by Joe Newbry and Chad Newbry

We're trying to build a network where people can communicate and discover the content of other people around them.

1. Basic premise: People find it valuable to view cards of the people around them.
2. Test by creating card viewer and chat for people within bluetooth range.
3. Goal to hit 1000 users by the end of the month.

Basic Hypothesis: A card viewer with chat for people within bluetooth range will hit 1000 users with two version released 2/10 2/20 by 2/28.
